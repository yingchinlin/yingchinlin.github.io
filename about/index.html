WHY I BLOGGING HERE?

While there are many internet companies offering blogging services, they more or less restrict individual freedom and rights, which I don’t like.So I had to give myself some necessary principles and specific rules.

I need and must truthfully record some of my experiences and thoughts for future reference. I will try to stay true to my ethical requirements as much as possible, while I will try to minimize external interference with my records.
