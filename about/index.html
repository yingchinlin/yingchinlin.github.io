SHARE A POEM I LIKE

The sail is whitening alone
In blue obscurity of sea:
What did it leave in country own?
What does it want so far to see.

The wind is strong, the mast is creaking,
The wave is playing with the wave …
But not a fortune is it seeking,
Nor from this fortune is its way.

By it a stream is bright as azure,
By beams of sun it’s warmed and blessed
But it is seeking gales as treasure,
As if the tempests give a rest.

From Mikhail Lermontov,The Sail.

WHY I BLOGGING HERE

Overthinking has been annoying me and I need a place to record, organize and share some of my daily thoughts. So I want to refine some principles, make some rules and build a life system for myself.

I need and must truthfully record some of my experiences and thoughts for future reference. I will try to stay true to my ethical requirements as much as possible, while I will try to minimize external interference with my records.
