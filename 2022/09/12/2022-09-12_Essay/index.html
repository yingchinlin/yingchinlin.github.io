Essay 2022-09-12

Author: Wilkin Lim Date: 2022-09-12  00:00:00 Category: 生活

Please note that this article was organized on 2022-09-12 and last modified on 2022-09-12, some of the information may be out of date.

When I was a kid, I always wanted to grow up, but when I grew up, I always wanted to go back to my childhood.

I have been thinking, exploring and answering endlessly about who I am, but none of the answers satisfy me. In my understanding, people are constantly inheriting, absorbing, updating and changing, and for people, what the words point to is always far more than what the words express.

Obviously, it is some very simple things that can be streamlined, but my company has not made it clear and easy to handle. It can be seen that the management ability of this company is very poor.
